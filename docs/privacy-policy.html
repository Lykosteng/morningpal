Overview

MorningPal ("the Extension") is a Chrome browser extension that helps users start their workday with personalized AI coaching. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

1. User-Provided Information

  • Account Information: Email address and password when you register
  • Conversation Data: Messages you exchange with the AI coach

2. Automatically Collected Information

  • Preferences: Your reminder time settings and notification preferences
  • Usage Statistics: Number of conversations and streak count

How We Use Your Information

  • Provide the Service: To deliver personalized AI coaching based on your daily state
  • Send Reminders: To notify you at your preferred time each day
  • Improve the Service: To understand usage patterns and enhance features

Data Storage

  • Local Storage: Your preferences and authentication tokens are stored locally in your browser using Chrome's storage API
  • Backend Server: Conversation data is stored on our secure backend server
  • No Third-Party Sharing: We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties

Permissions Used

Permission Purpose
storage Save your preferences and login state locally
alarms Schedule daily reminder notifications
notifications Send morning reminder alerts
host_permissions Connect to the backend API server

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • Passwords are hashed using bcrypt
  • API communications use secure protocols
  • Authentication uses JWT tokens

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Export your conversation history
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Opt out of notifications at any time

Data Retention

  • Your data is retained as long as your account is active
  • You can delete your account and all data at any time through the extension settings

Children's Privacy

MorningPal is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
